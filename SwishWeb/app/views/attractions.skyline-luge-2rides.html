Invented in New Zealand over 27 years ago*, and having hosted over 30 million rides* worldwide Skyline Luge Sentosa is the first-ever Luge introduced in Southeast Asia and has since hosted over 8 million rides!

2 types of experience:

  • Luge Ride - a unique 3 wheeled gravity ride that provides the rider full control over their descent on a concrete tracks.
  • Skyride – a 320 metre chairlift concept that allows you to enjoy the panoramic bird’s eye view of Sentosa island and also the Singapore city skyline. Each Luge ride can be anywhere from a few minutes for an adventurous rider to seven minutes for those who like to take it more leisurely. The Skyride ride in one direction lasts about seven minutes.

The Dragon Trail boasts an elevated course, which builds up excitement with its tighter corners and numerous turns. This track is designed with themed landscaping and night lighting to provide a mystical backdrop to the ride.

The Jungle Trail is more sheltered with mature trees as this was the original track built in 2005. It is slightly shorter than Dragon Trail and it has longer wider corners and long straights.

The total length combined is about 1400 metres.
The dragon trail which recently opened is 688 metres in length while the jungle trail is 628 metres.

The Skyride can take a maximum of 4 persons.

Restrictions

Luge is safe for all ages. Each track is 4-metres wide so there is sufficient space to manoeuvre. All Luge riders receive a full safety briefing from trained staff prior to their luge ride. Each rider is fully-instructed and made to wear a safety helmet before the ride commences.

To ride the Luge & Skyride, you must be in good health and free of heart conditions, motion sickness, epilepsy, back & neck problems and other physical limitations.

Expectant mothers are not allowed to ride.

Riders must be at least 110cm tall and at least 6 years of age to ride the Luge on their own, otherwise they can double up with an adult. To ride the Skyride, children must be over 85cm tall.

Height Requirements

Skyride – Min. 85 cm to ride with an adult and 135 cm to ride alone. 1 adult can only accompany 2 children.

Luge – 6 years and above or a minimum height of 110 cm to ride alone.
