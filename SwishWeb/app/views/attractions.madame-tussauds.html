Can guests re-enter with same day tickets or do they have to re-purchase?
The admission ticket is for one time use only. Should guests wish to re-enter the attraction after they exit the exit turnstile, they would need to purchase a new ticket.

For refund/change of date matters:
No refunds/exchanges permitted.

Am I allowed to eat/drinks within the attraction?
No. Guests are not permitted to eat/drinks within the attraction and including baby food.

Any nursing room/baby change table in the toilet?
No, the toilets do not include nursing room/baby change table.

Will there be planned guided tours?
No, we do not have guided tours as our attraction is self-guiding. Guest are welcome to wander around our attraction at their own pace.

Are all wax figure placed on the same level?
Yes, all wax figures in Madame Tussauds Singapore are placed on ground level and there’s relevant ramp to access.

Am I allowed to touch the figures?
Yes, at Madame Tussauds you can get up close and personal with celebrities, icons and historical leaders in a fully interactive experience.

What is the duration of the boat ride?
The boat ride duration is about 3 minutes.

What time is the first and last boat ride?
We open at 10am, the boat ride starts from 11am. Last admission into the attraction on Monday to Friday is at 6pm and on Saturday, Sunday & public holidays at 7:30pm, we’ll make sure all guests are able to board the boat ride.

Are there any height/age/weight restriction?
Those aged 12 years old and below must be accompanied by an adult. Children below the height of 1.4metres have to be accompanied by an adult during the ride. Please note that no babies in arms are allowed.

Are wheelchairs allowed on the boat ride?
No, the boat ride does not accommodate wheelchairs.

Can I take a pushchair or pram inside the attraction?
Families with young children are advised that baby prams are not allowed in the attraction. Prams must be left outside at the drop-off point.

Is the attraction wheelchair friendly?
Yes, our attraction is wheelchair friendly, however we can only accommodate 2 wheelchairs at one time for safety reasons. The Spirit of Singapore boat ride cannot accommodate wheelchairs or people with serious mobility issues.

I’m interested in having a wax hand made – what should I be aware of?
Wax hands are not recommended if you:

Have skin allergy
Have sensitive skin
Have broken skin
Dislike hot liquid
Are under the age of 5
Have high blood pressure
Are pregnant

By Sentosa Express

Head to the 3rd level of the VivoCity shopping mall for the Sentosa Express monorail to Sentosa Island.

Alight at Merlion Station, then head across Merlion Plaza to the escalators.

After 4 flights of escalators, you will see the red carpet of Madame Tussauds Singapore.

By Cable Car

From HarbourFront MRT take Exit B (HarbourFront Centre).

Follow directional signs leading to Singapore Cable Car station at HarbourFront Tower II and take the cable car to Sentosa Island.

By Foot

Take the MRT to HarbourFront station.

From VivoCity Shopping Mall’s waterfront promenade you can enter via the Sentosa Boardwalk.

This is a 30-minute walk.

Please follow directional signs to Madame Tussauds Singapore which is at Imbiah Lookout.
